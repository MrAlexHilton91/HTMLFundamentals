Hot Cocoa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 T Wired Brain Coffee Hot Cocoa Mix
  • 8 oz Milk
  • Marshmallows (optional)

Steps

  1. Scoop hot cocoa mix into a mug
  2. Heat milk on stove or microwave to just below boil
  3. Pour milk into mug stirring gently while you pour
  4. Stir until mix is fully dissolved into milk
  5. Top with marshmallows

Cocoa Facts

Do you know the difference?
Cocoa
The powdered substance left after processing cacao beans and has little or no cocoa butter
Chocolate
Basically cocoa with the cocoa butter and often sugar and milk
